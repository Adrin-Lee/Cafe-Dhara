Our New Journey

Welcome to Café Dhara

A new destination in Bengaluru filled with warm brews, delicious bites, and memorable moments.

Inspired by the Sanskrit word "Dhara", which signifies the steady, nourishing flow of the Earth, Café Dhara is built as a nature-infused sanctuary. Set inside an architectural double-story garden framework, we combine organic greenery, ambient light, and rustic wooden textures.

We source our specialty coffee beans from the mist-covered hills of Chikmagalur, roasted to perfection to ensure a refreshing and rich flavor profile in every cup.

Join us on our opening day to experience our tranquil space, meet our passionate baristas, and enjoy complimentary hospitality as we begin this new journey together.
